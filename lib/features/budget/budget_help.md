# Team Budget Help

This page explains how to use the Actual Budget integration in Scout.

## What is Actual Budget?
Actual Budget is an open-source collaborative budgeting tool. It helps teams track spending, plan budgets, and manage finances together.

## How to Use
1. Click the **Open Actual Budget** button in Scout to launch the app.
2. Log in or create an account if prompted.
3. Follow your team workflow for entering transactions, reviewing budgets, and collaborating.

## Official Documentation
For detailed help, see the [Actual Budget Docs](https://actualbudget.org/docs/).

## Tips for Chaplain Teams
- Use categories to track spending by intervention, resource, or department.
- Review budget reports regularly to ensure stewardship and transparency.
- Collaborate on budget planning for upcoming spiritual care initiatives.

If you need more help, ask your team lead or visit the official docs above.
